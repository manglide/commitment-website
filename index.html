About

Have you paid for a product that was never delivered, or you delivered a product and payment was never made? If that's true, then you need the commitment app. The commitment app was built to solve trust issues in business transactions. We all visit social media and therefore come in contact with so many merchants selling products, items and services. You have to really trust someone to pay upfront without getting the goods. If lucky, the fellow honours, if not, your funds is lost. With the commitment app, you create that business transaction on the app and then invite the second party. You make your commitment to the deal on the app while the 2nd party get's updated. If he goes along and fulfills his own obligation, he can return to the app and request payment. Payment is finally disbursed at the initiators final approval.

FAQ

What is the Commitment App?
Commitment App helps facilitate business deal payments. Lets see an Example: Femi sells shoes and is based in Kaduna. Emmanuel wants to buy some of the shoes and so he contacts Femi via Instagram. Femi says he cant send the shoes until Emma makes payment. Emma is scared and wants Femi to send down the shoes first before payment. Emma cancels out for fear and Femi looses a market. With the commitment App, Emma creates a business deal on the App and ties Femis account & mobile number plus business description to the deal been created. Emma pays the commitment amount required by Femi through the App. This money doesnt go to Femi directly, it sits on the App, but Femi gets notified that Emma has made payment commitment. Femi sends the shoes and and requests final disbursement from the App. Emma gets notified and authorizes. Money gets sent to Femi.
Which payment processor do you use?
We use Flutterwave. Please visit their website https://flutterwave.com to know more about them.
How much are your charges?
We charge a flat fee of N5 plus the associated transfer fees as stipulated by CBN.
  1. For transactions below N5,000, we charge N15.00
  2. For transactions between N5,001 and N50,000, we charge N30.00
  3. For transactions above N50,000, we charge N55.00
When are the charges deducted?
On final disbursement to receiving party.
Who bears the cost?
Since this is deducted from the business deal value, the creator bears the cost.
What happens if I cancel the deal?
No cost is deducted and your funds are automatically transferred back to you.
How fast can i receive my payment
Instantly.
As an initiator, if a dispute is filed against the deal what happens?
When a dispute is initiated by either the recipient or initiator, funds are frozen until mediation occurs and a level playing background is established amongst parties.
