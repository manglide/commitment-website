What is the Commitment App

Commitment App helps facilitate business deal payments. We are like middle men players. We handle the vital part of any transaction. Lets see an Example: Femi sells shoes and is based in Kaduna. Emmanuel wants to buy some of the shoes and so he contacts Femi via Instagram. Femi says he cant send the shoes until Emma makes payment. Emma is scared and wants Femi to send down the shoes first before payment. Emma cancels out for fear and Femi looses a market. With the commitment App, Emma creates a business deal on the App and ties Femis account & mobile number plus business description to the deal been created. Emma pays the commitment amount required by Femi through the App. This money doesnt go to Femi directly, it sits on the App, but Femi gets notified that Emma has made payment commitment. Femi sends the shoes and and requests final disbursement from the App. Emma gets notified and authorizes. Money gets sent to Femi.

Charges we Collect

We charge N5 flat rate across board. The CBN new transfer rates states that

  • For transfers below N5,000, N10 is the charge, adding our N5 flat rate makes it N15 for transactions below N5,000
  • For transfers above N5,000 but below N50,000 N20 is the charge, adding our N5 flat rate makes it N25 for transactions in this range
  • For transfers above N50,000, N50 is the charge, adding our N5 flat rate makes it N55 for transactions above N50,000
