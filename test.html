Subject: Mathematics  

Topic: Part of a circle

Duration: 40mins

Reference Material: Macmillan champion primary mathematics book 5 

Previous knowledge: Pupils are familiar with plane shapes

Objectives: At the end of the lesson, pupil should:

  • Find the radius of a circle
  • Calculate the diameter of a circle

Content:

 

Example 1: Find the radius of a circle with diameter 8cm

r = d/2                 d = 8cm

r = d/2       =        8cm/2 =4cm

Therefore r =4cm

Example 2: Find the diameter of a circle with radius 5cm

d = 2r, since r plus r = 2r = d (diameter is twice as long as the radius)

d = 2 x 5m = 10m therefore d = 10m

Presentation:  

Step 1: Teacher revises plane shape with the pupil

Step 2: Teacher introduce and explain part of a circle to the pupil

Step 3: Pupil are allowed to ask question

Step 4: Teacher give pupil question to solve   

Evaluation:

  1. Find the radius of a circle with the following diameter

(a) 4cm  (b) 10cm  (c) 8cm  (d) 21cm  (e) 6cm (f) 9cm   (g) 100cm (h) 38cm

  1. Find the diameter of a circle with the following radius

(a) 1cm (b) 7½m (c) 7cm (d) 5½m (e) 5m (f) 4.5cm

Conclusion: Teacher moves round for inspection, marking and correction of notes where necessary
